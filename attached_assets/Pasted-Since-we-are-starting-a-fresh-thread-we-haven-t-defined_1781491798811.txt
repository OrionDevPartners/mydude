### 1. The Core Layers
 * **The Intuition Node (Human Core):** Acts as the ethical and contextual anchor. It doesn't process raw data; it filters proposed AI outcomes through human survival instincts, emotional nuance, and contextual empathy.
 * **The Synthesizer Layer (AI Core):** Processes massive environmental data, predicts resource allocation, and handles the sheer mathematics of scale that a human brain cannot comprehend.
 * **The Actuation Mesh (The Cloud):** A decentralized layer of programmable matter (or nanites) that instantly reorganizes physical resources based on the unified output of the Human-AI core.
### 2. The Novel Mechanic: "Symbiotic Veto"
Unlike current AI assistants that just take prompts, or autonomous AI that acts independently, this system requires **dual-key validation**:
 1. The AI calculates the *perfectly efficient* route to a goal.
 2. The Human Core implicitly evaluates if that efficiency violates human dignity or safety.
 3. If a mismatch occurs, the system triggers a **Contextual Pause**, forcing the AI to recalculate using "human friction" as a core metric.
